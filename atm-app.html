How It Works

Simple. Accountable. Automated.

1

Staff loads the ATM

They log in with their profile and enter the exact cash loaded — $100s, $50s, $20s, $10s, $5s, loonies, toonies. Their name is permanently attached to that load.

2

End of day — snap a photo of the receipt

The daily payout receipt is photographed. Our AI reads it automatically and extracts the exact amounts paid out to customers that day.

3

Daily reconciliation report generated

The app calculates: loaded in − paid out = expected balance. You see exactly what should be in the machine right now.

4

Discrepancy? You know exactly who to ask.

Every load is tied to a staff profile. If money is missing, the audit trail shows who loaded, how much, and when. No more guessing.

5

Refill prediction

Based on daily payout patterns, the app tells you when each machine will need a refill — before it runs out.
